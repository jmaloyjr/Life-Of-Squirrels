How To Play

In this game, you are a squirrel traveling up trees, trying to avoid obstacles.

To do this, you will switch between trees by pressing either the left or right arrow keys.

You will start the game with 3 hearts (hitting branches or construction cones will deduct one heart, while hitting a hawk will result in an instant game over).

The more acorns or Chick-Fil-A sandwiches you get, the more points you will collect in the game. These points can be exchanged for customizable skins.
