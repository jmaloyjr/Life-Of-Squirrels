How To Play

In this game, you are a squirrel traveling up trees, trying to avoid obstacles.

To do this, you will switch between trees by pressing either the left or right arrow keys.

You will start the game with 3 hearts (hitting branches or construction cones will deduct one heart, while hitting a hawk will instantly end the game).

The more acorns you get, the more points you will collect in the game. These points can be exchanged for customizable skins. If you happen to collect a Golden Chick-Fil-A Sandwich TM, one heart will be regenerated.
